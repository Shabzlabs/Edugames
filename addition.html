About Addition Jungle

Addition Jungle is an interactive math game that helps young learners practise addition facts through a playful jungle-themed challenge. Students solve addition equations, type their answers using a number pad, and move through progressively harder levels while earning bananas for correct responses.

What Students Learn

Students practise one-digit addition, adding a two-digit number from 10 to 20 with a one-digit number, and adding two-digit numbers. The game supports number sense, mental math, fact fluency, place value awareness, and confidence with multi-step practice.

How to Play

Read the addition question on the card, enter the answer using the number pad, and press GO. Correct answers flip the card to reveal the solution and move the student forward. Students can continue through ten questions per level and complete all three levels to finish the game.

For Teachers and Parents

This activity can be used for math centres, independent practice, tutoring, early finishers, or extra support for students who need repeated practice with addition facts. The immediate feedback helps students correct mistakes and stay engaged.

Learning Goals

Learners strengthen addition fluency, improve accuracy, practise using numbers confidently, and build persistence through short, repeated challenges. The level structure helps students move from simple facts toward more challenging addition problems.
