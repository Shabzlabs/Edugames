Word Count Studio Help & Information

How metrics are counted:

  • Words: Separated by sequence characters and white spaces.
  • Sentences: Defined by punctuation marks like (., ?, !).
  • Paragraphs: Identified by continuous hard breaks or double line breaks.
  • Stop Words: Common auxiliary words filtered from densities so you identify key theme topics.
