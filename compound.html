What Are Compound Words?

Compound words are made when two smaller words join together to create a new word with a new meaning. For example, sun and flower become sunflower.

What Students Practise

Students practise reading, vocabulary, word meaning, spelling patterns, and choosing the correct compound word based on visual clues.

How to Play

Look carefully at the two pictures or symbols. Think about the words they represent, then choose the compound word that combines both ideas.

For Teachers and Parents

This activity can be used for literacy centres, vocabulary review, word study, independent practice, or a quick warm-up before a reading lesson.
