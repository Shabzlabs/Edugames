3D Shapes for Kids

Learn before playing Roll or Stack!

What Are 3D Shapes?

3D shapes are solid objects that take up space. Unlike flat shapes, 3D shapes have length, width, and height. You can hold them, stack them, or roll them.

You see 3D shapes every day, like balls, boxes, cans, and ice cream cones.

Common 3D Shapes

Cube

All sides are equal. Looks like a dice.

Rectangular Prism

A box shape. Like a cereal box.

Sphere

Perfectly round. Like a ball.

Cylinder

Has flat circles and can roll.

Cone

Pointed top and round base.

Square-based pyramid

Pointed top and a square base.

Triangular Prism

Has triangle ends and rectangle sides.

Roll, Stack, or Both?

Roll: Shapes with curved surfaces roll (sphere, cylinder, cone).

Stack: Shapes with flat surfaces stack (cube, rectangular prism).

Both: Some shapes can do both!

Shapes in Real Life

Look around your home or classroom. You can find cubes in blocks, spheres in balls, cylinders in cans, and cones in ice cream cones.

Try this: Find one object that rolls and one that stacks!
