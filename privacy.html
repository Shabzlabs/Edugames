Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ShabzLabs handles information when visitors use this website. ShabzLabs is an educational website that provides interactive learning games and resources for students, parents, teachers, and other visitors.

Information We Collect

ShabzLabs does not directly collect personal information from visitors through account registration, sign-ups, or login forms. Visitors can use the games and learning resources without creating an account.

If a visitor chooses to contact ShabzLabs by email, any information included in that email may be used only to respond to the message or address the question, suggestion, or technical issue.

Cookies and Third-Party Services

This website may use third-party services such as Google AdSense in the future to display advertisements. These services may use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies to show ads, measure performance, and improve advertising relevance.

Cookies are small files stored on a user’s device. They may help third-party services understand how visitors interact with websites or help provide more relevant advertisements.

Google AdSense and Advertising

Google, as a third-party vendor, may use cookies to serve ads on this website. Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on a visitor’s visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet.

Visitors may choose to opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google’s Ads Settings page.

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to maintain the website, improve learning resources, respond to questions, fix technical issues, and support website functionality. ShabzLabs does not sell personal information.

Children’s Privacy

ShabzLabs is designed to support educational learning for primary students. The website does not require children to create accounts, enter names, or provide personal information in order to use the games.

Parents, guardians, and teachers are encouraged to supervise children’s use of online educational resources.

External Links

This website may contain links to other pages or third-party services. ShabzLabs is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external websites.

Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as the website grows or as new features are added. Any updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact ShabzLabs through the contact page.
