Privacy Policy

How ShabzLabs Handles Information

This Privacy Policy explains how ShabzLabs handles information when visitors use this website, including learning games, educational resources, contact links, and advertising services.

Last updated: May 23, 2026
🔍

Information We Collect

ShabzLabs does not require visitors to create accounts, sign in, or enter personal information to use the learning games and resources.

If a visitor chooses to email ShabzLabs, the information included in that email may be used to reply to the message, respond to feedback, or address a technical issue.

🍪

Cookies

ShabzLabs may use cookies or similar technologies through third-party services, including Google AdSense, to support advertising, website functionality, ad measurement, and security.

Cookies are small files stored on a visitor’s device. Visitors can adjust cookie settings through their browser.

📢

Google AdSense

Google and other third-party vendors may use cookies to serve ads based on a visitor’s prior visits to this website or other websites.

Google’s use of advertising cookies enables Google and its partners to serve ads based on visits to this site and/or other sites on the internet.

👧

Children’s Privacy

ShabzLabs is designed to support educational learning for primary students. The website does not require children to create accounts, enter names, or provide personal information to use the games.

Parents, guardians, and teachers are encouraged to supervise children’s use of online educational resources.

Third-Party Advertising and Data Use

Third-party vendors, including Google, may place and read cookies on visitors’ browsers or use web beacons, IP addresses, or similar technologies to collect information as a result of ad serving on this website.

These technologies may be used to provide advertisements, limit how often an ad is shown, measure ad performance, prevent fraud and abuse, and improve advertising relevance.

Visitors may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google’s Ads Settings. Visitors may also learn more about how Google uses information from sites or apps that use Google services by reviewing Google’s privacy and advertising information.

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to:

  • Maintain and improve the ShabzLabs website.
  • Respond to questions, feedback, or technical issues.
  • Improve educational resources and learning activities.
  • Display, measure, and manage advertising if ads are added to the website.
  • Protect the website from spam, abuse, or technical problems.

ShabzLabs does not sell personal information.

External Links

ShabzLabs may contain links to other pages or third-party services. ShabzLabs is not responsible for the privacy practices, policies, or content of external websites. Visitors should review the privacy policies of any external websites they choose to visit.

Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as ShabzLabs grows, adds new features, or uses new services. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Please review this page periodically for changes.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact ShabzLabs through the contact page.
